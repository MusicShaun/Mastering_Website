About Me

Producer, Mastering Engineer

I completed a Bachelors degree at WAAPA in 2010.
Since then, I produced as COMMAND Q and Monsoon Moon, with tracks listed on labels such as Skrillex's NESTHQ and Diplo's Mad Decent.
I've been mastering for labels and inviduals of all backgrounds for the last 6 years.
I specialise in trap, dubstep, indie-electronic and electronic based music. My signature sound is loud and clean, which is important when you want to stand out in a club enrivonment.
My clients are too many to list, so head over to the examples tab to hear my masters.
